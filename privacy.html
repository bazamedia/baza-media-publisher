Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Baza Media Publisher ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Create an account or use our services
  • Connect your TikTok account to our platform
  • Upload content for publishing
  • Contact us for support

2. Types of Data Collected

Personal Data:

  • Email address
  • TikTok account information (username, user ID)
  • Authentication tokens for TikTok API access

Usage Data:

  • Publishing history and timestamps
  • Application performance data
  • Error logs and debugging information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Authenticate and authorize your TikTok API access
  • Publish content to TikTok on your behalf
  • Communicate with you about service updates
  • Ensure compliance with TikTok's terms of service
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With TikTok: To authenticate and publish content via their API
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure API token storage

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own privacy policy and terms of service.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
